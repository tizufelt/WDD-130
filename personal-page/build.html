How to get a hold of us

a Project

Get in touch with us and let us know what your intentions for the project are. We do anything from tune-ups to full restorations. If we are able and willing to take on the project, you can ship the vehicle our way so it can join the wait list. Your project isn’t on the wait list until it’s at our shop.

Project Start

We have a wait list, so it is likely we won’t start work on your car immediately. We do not charge for your vehicle to sit on our lot. Once the project reaches the top of the wait list, we will get in contact with you to be sure you are ready for work to begin.

Work & Billing

We only bill you when work is happening on your project. If you are unable to pay for work, we will stop working until you are able to pay. Cost is $85 per man-hour plus parts and supplies, billed out on Thursdays.

Turn-Around Time

the rustiest projects have between a 3-to-6-month turnaround time, but work is only done when both money and parts are available.

Final Delivery

You may drive your car away from shop when its ready, but if we’re too far away you can always hire a vehicle delivery driver to pick up the car and we will make sure it gets on the truck safely.
