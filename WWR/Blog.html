A great time for all!

We always have a great time when we hit the river for some rapids. When you book your trip we include lunch and as you can see we have fun. The group is heading to the water and the boats tend to be on the heavy side so it takes all of us to carry the boat to the water. Once in the water there is no telling the ammount of wet you will get, so be prepared to see the river up close and personal. Of cours we are safe. We require all passengers to wear a life vest and helmet for their protection. So come join us for a fun adventure that you won't soo forget.
